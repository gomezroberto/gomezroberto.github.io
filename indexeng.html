Furvo!

FURVO! is an app designed to organize amateur soccer games in 3 simple steps: 1. An organizer creates the game in "New Game". 2. Using the share button, they send the link via WhatsApp to individuals or groups. 3. Each invitee enters the game with that link and confirms attendance.
